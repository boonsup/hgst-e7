\section*{Appendix F: Grade-Scalar Sensitivity}
\label{app:sensitivity}

\addcontentsline{toc}{section}{Appendix F: Grade-Scalar Sensitivity}

Numerical evaluation with bases $p\in\{2,3,e\}$ at $L=8$, $\beta=8.0$ gives
$R_\infty$ variation $<1\%$ across all groups, confirming that the qualitative
ordering results are independent of base choice; the analytical isomorphism
$G_\Delta \cong (\mathbb{Z},+)$ established in Definition~\ref{def:lattice}
(any $b>1$ yields the same isomorphism class) provides the formal underpinning.
